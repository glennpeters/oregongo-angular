Portland Go Tournament Fall 2004

Date: October 30-31, 2004

Time:

  • 9 AM - 6 PM (Saturday) (a few may be playing past 6 PM)
  • 9 AM - 4:30 PM (Sunday)

Please show up by 9am to register. It takes some time to get everything together, but having everyone show up on time will help things go along smoothly.

Where: Reed College, Vollum Lounge
Map: Vollum Hall is above the traffic circle in the center

What: 5 rounds of handicap Go, Ing rules, AGA rated

Cost: $25 if pre-registered, $35 at the door. (Pre-registered players may still pay at the door.) Current Reed students and those under 18 get in free.

And a reminder that everyone participating needs to be a current AGA member. You can pay an additional $10 at the door for a limited membership.

We have three prize divisions:

  • dan
  • single digit kyu
  • double digit kyu

Please pre-register -- it will make the process much easier. Pre-registration closes on October 29.

To pre-register, or for additional info, contact Glenn Peters.

To pre-register, please provide the following information (if not, I'll just have to ask for it):

  • Your name
  • Your rating (7 ku, 2 dan, etc. AGA rating if possible, otherwise estimate)
  • AGA Membership status (current, lapsed, non-member -- you'll need an AGA membership for the tournament whether or not you pre-register.)
  • If you need an AGA membership, you will also need to provide a mailing address.

2009 Salem Winter Tournament

What

It is a one day tournament with three games. Handicap stones will be given as needed. Prizes and cash will be given for various divisions including dan, single digit kyu, and double digit kyu.

  • Cash prizes!
  • Door prizes!
  • Free youth entry (with AGA membership)
  • Special deal! - bring a first time tournament player and get $5 off your tournament entry fee

When:

Saturday, February 21, 2009. Doors will be open at 8:30 AM. Pairings will be made at 9:30. Anyone registering after 9:00 will be given a bye for the first round.

Where:

Tokyo International University of America (TIUA)
1300 Mill Street, room #144
Directions: Mapquest | Google Maps

Preregistration is $20 for 18 and older. Free to anyone under 18. If you wait to register the day of the tournament, the fee will be $25.

To register, please contact Cynthia Gaty with your name, AGA number, rank.

Lunch will be on your own with several restaurant options nearby.

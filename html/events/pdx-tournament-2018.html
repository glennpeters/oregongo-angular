Portland Tournament 2018

January 13-14, 2018

You can register on-line now. (More information about cost, etc. this link.)

This is the annual (though belated) Portland Tournament, a five-round tournament with both open and handicap sections. A minimum rating of 1-dan (AGA) is required for entry in the open section. The open section will be limited to the top six players by official AGA rating. Open section games will be played even, with 7.5 points komi. In the handicap section, full handicaps will be used.

Portland Fall Tournament - 10/12-13/02

Date: October 12 & 13, 2002

Time: 9 AM - 6 PM (Saturday) (a few may be playing past 6 PM)
9 AM - 4:30 PM (Sunday)

Where: Reed College, Vollum Lounge (See map -- Vollum Hall is just north of the circle in the center of the map)

What: 5 rounds of handicap Go, Ing rules, AGA rated

Cost: $20 kyu players, $25 dan players if pre-registering (an additional $10 if not pre-registered). Send no money at this time; it will be collected at the door.

We have four prize divisions:

  • open (depends on how many enter)
  • dan
  • single digit kyu
  • double digit kyu

These divisions are for prizes only, and do not limit pairings.

Register: Contact Glenn Peters at glenn@aenigma.com (Please pre-register; it makes a lot of things easier in the preparation. Pre-registration closes on Thursday, October 4.)

Portland Fall Open

When:
Sat & Sun, Oct 21st & 22nd

Where:
Reed College Portland, OR

How:
$20 entrance fee if you preregister (easy)
$30 accepted at the door if you haven't preregistered
AGA membership required

We plan a four division event, an Open and a Dan section, playing five games over two days, one hour on the clock and five 30 second periods of byo-yomi using the Ing Timers. The two kyu divisions will be seven games over two days, using chess clocks so they can use the more familiar 25 stones in 10 mins byo-yomi. We expect in excess of 50 players this year.

Divisions:

  • open
  • dan
  • single digit kyu
  • double digit kyu
and right now we have players registered from 24 kyu up to 6 dan.

And, please bring your boards (stones and timers supplied)

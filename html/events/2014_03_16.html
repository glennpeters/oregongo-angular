The Hikaru No Go Children and Youth Go Tournament

Cash Prizes for Winners!

For children and youth, unranked up to 14 kyu.

Preregistration Required: To register post here that you are planning to play with your (youth) or your child's name, age, and rank if any, by March 2nd.

Date: Sunday, March 16th

Place: Taborspace, located at 54th and SE Belmont, Portland, OR (Mt.Tabor Church)

Time: Play begins at 1 p.m., ends by 4 p.m.

Tournament Details:

Play at least either:

  • 4 9*9 games
  • 3 13*13 games
  • 2 19*19 games
  • or 3 games of any combination of the above

Registration Fee, Payable at the door: $2.00

Scholarships available.

Go Workshop with Janice Kim

When: Saturday, September 8 and Sunday, September 9, 2001
Where: Cheldelin Middle School Library 987 NE Conifer Blvd Corvallis, Oregon
Hours: 9 am to 6 pm, each day
Cost: $125 to cover both days / $70 for Saturday only

Sponsored by the Corvallis Go Club

We expect a diverse range of playing strengths to be present. We are considering having informal matches on the side, game reviews, and lectures over the two-day workshop. To help focus the content for the different players, we will likely break up into groups of different playing strengths.

We hope that you can participate in this two-day workshop. However, if you cannot participate on both days, you might be able to make it for one day. You may register for Saturday only if you wish ($70).

Getting to Cheldelin Middle School Library (987 NE Conifer Blvd.) Coming from the North or the South on I-5, continue until you get to Hwy 34, the Corvallis-Lebanon exit. Take that exit and go west (11 miles) on Hwy 34 until you get to Corvallis. Be sure you are in the right-hand lane as you cross the small bridge into Corvallis; you need to turn right (north) just as you get over the bridge. You are now on Hwy 20. Continue north along the river until the 2nd stopight (Conifer Blvd). Turn left (west) on Conifer Blvd. Cheldelin is on Conifer, just past the railroad tracks. You can park in the bus lane, or in the parking lot. The entrance we will be using is near the flag pole (on the south side, near the bus lane). Signs will be posted.

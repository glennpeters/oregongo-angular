Ing Rules

Ing rules allow for:

  • free placement of handicap stones
  • use of 8.5 pt komi in even games
  • a special case ko rule
  • a variation on chinese counting (which uses starting with the correct number of stones)

The ko rule will be discussed before the tournament, and the scoring style will be review. the largest difference is free placement of handicap stones, but you may prefer to just use hoshi points.

This tournament is sponsored by the Corvallis Go Club AGA chapter). The tournament directors will also participate as players.

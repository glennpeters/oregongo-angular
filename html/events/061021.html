Portland Go Tournament 2006

Lewis & Clark College, Albany Quadrangle building
Portland, Oregon
(Google Maps location)
October 21-22

We've significantly reduced the entry fees. It will be $20, $10 if you preregister. (If you are not an AGA member, there is also a $10 one-tournament membership fee). Lewis & Clark students/faculty/staff and players under 16 get in free.

Prizes will be offered for:

  • Top three dan players
  • Top three single-digit kyu players
  • Top three double-digit kyu players
  • Top youth player (under 16)
  • Top female player
  • Top new AGA member (must purchase a full membership)
  • Best costume (day 2)

If you plan to attend, send me (drake@lclark.edu) your name and rank.

Play Go

"Minutes to learn, a lifetime to master"

Go is an ancient board game (between three to five thousand years old), usually played on a wooden board with black and white stones. (We call them stones, but they can be made of any material.) The rules are simple, but the potential for play is virtually endless.

See the American Go Association's What is Go? page for a deeper description of the game.

Recommended books for beginners:

  • Learn to Play Go, Janice Kim (with board)
  • Go for Beginners, Kaoru Iwamoto
  • Complete Introduction to Go, Cho Chikun
  • The Book of Go, William Cobb (with board)
  • The Elementary Go Series (7 volumes, formerly published by Ishi press, now published by Kiseido)
